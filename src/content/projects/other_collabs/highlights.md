At [LINQS](https://linqs.stanford.edu/) I've participated in a number of fruitful collaborations as a supporting member.
* Efficient frequency-modulated integrated combs: [_Nature_, *627*, 95 (2024)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-024-07071-2). I contributed to the initial chip design and fabrication.
* Gain-trapped solitons: [_Optica_, *11*, 315 (2024)](https://doi.org/10.1364/OPTICA.510591). I led the fabrication, and participated in theoretical modeling, chip design, and measurements.
* Ultrabroadband chip-based mid-infrared source: [_Optics Express_, *30*, 32752 (2022)](https://doi.org/10.1364/OE.467580). I led the fabrication, and contributed to the chip design.
* Three-octave chip-based UV-to-MIR supercontinuum: [_Optics Express_, *32*, 12004 (2024)](https://doi.org/10.1364/OE.514649). I led the fabrication, and contributed to the chip design.
* Several others that I don't have space to list here! See my [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=0g3wHc0AAAAJ&hl=en) page.
